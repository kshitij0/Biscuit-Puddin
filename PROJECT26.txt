Biscuit Pudding

INGREDEINTS

Hide n seek biscuit(2 packets)
Amul cream/Fesh cream(250g)
Sugar(6-7 spoons)
Coffee powder(1/2 spoon)
Warm water(1/2 glass)

PROCESS

Make a solution of coffee and water
Mix sugar in the cream 

*Use a glass container 

Dip the biscuits one by one in coffee solution
Arrange the biscuits in rows and columns
Spread the mixture of cream and sugar on the biscuits
Repeat the process by making more layers of the biscuits
Deep freeze it for 3-4 hours

Now the pudding is ready to eat.
